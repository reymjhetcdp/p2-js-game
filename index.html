COSMIC COMBAT: Starship Annihilation

At any cost, we must defend our planet by thwarting the invasion of two distinct types of extraterrestrial life forms, one originating from an icy planet and the other from a blazing planet. Our mission is to eliminate their warships and ensure the safety of Earth. We must do whatever it takes to accomplish this goal.

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Move using the 'Arrow' or 'W A S D' keys.

2. Shoot bullets using the 'Spacebar'.

3. Enemies will appear randomly in pairs.

4. Each time you eliminate an enemy, your score will increase by 200.

5. Colliding with an enemy will result in a loss of HP, so strive to survive for as long as possible!
